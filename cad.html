7-Day CAD Project

Project Timeline: Completed in 7 Days

Introduction

This project was completed within a tight 7-day schedule, showcasing dedication, efficiency, and advanced Computer Aided Design (CAD) skills. The compressed timeline pushed for focused work and rapid iteration, resulting in a high-quality design delivered on time.

Design Process

The design journey followed a clear and structured workflow: starting with initial concept sketches and brainstorming, moving into detailed 3D modeling, and culminating in the final CAD output ready for review and production.

Key Learnings & Tools Used

  • Mastered rapid prototyping techniques to meet tight deadlines.
  • Enhanced precision and detail in 3D modeling.
  • Improved workflow efficiency through iterative design.
  • Tools/Software: AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Fusion 360.

Day 1: Introduction to Fusion 360 interface, navigation tools, and sketch environment. Day 2: Practiced creating 2D sketches and applying parametric constraints. Day 3: Learned 3D modeling features such as extrude, revolve, loft, and sweep. Day 4: Focused on creating assemblies and understanding motion joints. Day 5: Explored material application, appearance settings, and rendering basics. Day 6: Designed a mini-project (simple mechanical component) integrating sketches and assemblies. Day 7: Completed rendering, documentation, and reviewed the design workflow.

This project highlights the ability to deliver quality CAD designs efficiently within short timelines without compromising on detail or accuracy.
